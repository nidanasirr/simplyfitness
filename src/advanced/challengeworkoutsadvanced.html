Challenge Workouts

1. HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

Perform each exercise for 45 seconds with 15 seconds of rest in between. Repeat the circuit 3-4 times.

  • Burpees
  • Jump Squats
  • Mountain Climbers
  • Box Jumps
  • Plank Jacks

2. Strength and Power Circuit

Complete 5 rounds of the following exercises with minimal rest between sets:

  • Deadlifts: 6 reps at 80% of your one-rep max
  • Weighted Pull-Ups: 8 reps
  • Barbell Thrusters: 10 reps
  • Handstand Push-Ups: 5 reps (or modified pike push-ups)
  • Medicine Ball Slams: 15 reps

3. Cardio and Core Blast

Perform each exercise for 60 seconds, then rest for 15 seconds before moving to the next. Repeat the circuit 3 times.

  • Jump Rope (double unders if possible)
  • Plank with Alternating Leg Lifts
  • Battle Rope Waves
  • Russian Twists with Medicine Ball
  • Box Jumps (or step-ups for added difficulty)

4. Endurance Challenge

Run, bike, or row for 45 minutes at a moderate to high intensity. Try to maintain a consistent pace throughout the workout, pushing yourself to your limits.
