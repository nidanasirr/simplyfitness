Workout Routines

The following are examples of workout routines that you can do as a beginner according to a 6-day split but you can adjust them acccording to the split you prefer. In order to see how these workouts work you can go to the 'How to use equipment' section and the 'Workout From Home' section.

Beginner Home Workout Routine (6 day split)

Day Workout Focus Exercises (Sets x Reps)
Day 1: Push Upper Body (Push) Push-ups (3x8-12), Chair Dips (3x8-12), Pike Push-ups (2x6-10)
Day 2: Pull Upper Body (Pull) Doorway Rows (3x8-12), Towel Bicep Curls (3x8-12), Reverse Snow Angels (2x10-15)
Day 3: Legs Lower Body Squats (3x8-12), Lunges (3x8-12 per leg), Glute Bridges (3x10-15)
Upper Body (Push) Incline Push-ups (3x8-12), Tricep Dips (3x8-12), Shoulder Taps (2x10-15 per side)
Day 5: Pull Upper Body (Pull) Backpack Rows (3x8-12), Single-arm Towel Rows (3x8-12 per arm), Supermans (3x10-15)
Day 6: Legs Lower Body Wall Sits (3x30-60 seconds), Step-ups (3x8-12 per leg), Calf Raises (3x12-15)
Day 7 Rest Rest or light stretching/yoga

Beginner Gym Workout Routine (6 day split)

Day Workout Focus Exercises (Sets x Reps)
Day 1: Push Upper Body (Push) Bench Press (3x8-12), Dumbbell Shoulder Press (3x8-12), Tricep Pushdown (3x10-15)
Day 2: Pull Upper Body (Pull) Lat Pulldowns (3x8-12), Seated Cable Rows (3x8-12), Hammer Curls (3x10-15)
Day 3: Legs Lower Body Leg Press (3x8-12), Dumbbell Lunges (3x8-12 per leg), Leg Curls (3x10-15)
Day 4: Push Upper Body (Push) Incline Bench Press (3x8-12), Dumbbell Flyes (3x8-12), Overhead Dumbbell Extension (3x10-15)
Day 5: Pull Upper Body (Pull) Pull-ups (Assisted if needed) (3x6-8), Barbell Rows (3x8-12), Bicep Curls (3x10-15)
Day 6: Legs Lower Body Squats (3x8-12), Deadlifts (3x8-12), Standing Calf Raises (3x12-15)
Day 7 Rest Rest or light stretching/yoga
