Supplements

Supplement Benefits Recommended Usage
Protein Powders (Whey, Casein) Aids muscle repair and growth; supports overnight recovery. Post-workout (whey), Before bed (casein)
Branched-Chain Amino Acids (BCAAs) Reduces soreness, accelerates recovery, supports growth. Before/during/after workouts
Creatine Monohydrate Improves strength, power output, and muscle mass. Daily, with a loading phase if preferred
Beta-Alanine Increases endurance, delays fatigue. Pre-workout
Fish Oil (Omega-3 Fatty Acids) Supports heart, cognitive, and joint health; anti-inflammatory. With meals
Vitamin D Essential for bone health, immune function, and well-being. With meals
Magnesium Supports muscle function, energy production, better sleep. Before bed
Caffeine Enhances focus, increases energy, improves performance. Pre-workout
Multivitamin Fills nutritional gaps, supports overall health. With meals
Pre-Workout Supplements Increases energy, focus, and endurance. Pre-workout
