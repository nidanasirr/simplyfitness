Personal Experience

Hi! My name is Nida Nasir, I am 23 years old and in my final semester of university. I am pursuing an undergrad degree in computer science and am very eager to share my fitness journy with you!

I started going to the gym at the age of sixteen and started off with cardio as my goals at the time were to lose fat and increase my endurance. Eventually I got into personal training when my dad wanted to gain some muscle and I decided to be his workout buddy. We trained with a personal training 2-3 times a week and quicky started getting into the world of fitness.

I have spent years and years going through different workout routines, workout splits, moving from beginner to intermediate and eventually to advanced and have struggled with gym anxiety for years.
